## 3. Key Features Section

### Feature 1: Permanent Brand Intelligence
**Icon:** Brain with infinity symbol
**Headline:** `Your Brand DNA, Stored Forever`
**Description:** Complete a 5-minute brand profile once. FashionOS analyzes your website, social presence, and positioning—then remembers it forever. 

### Feature 2: One Shoot → All Channels
**Icon:** Camera splitting into multiple channel icons
**Headline:** `Auto-Route Every Asset`
**Description:** Plan one photoshoot. FashionOS's Creative Director AI knows what each platform needs—square crops for Instagram, vertical for TikTok, white-background for Shopify, lifestyle for your website.

**Icon:** Circular arrow with upward trend
**Headline:** `AI That Gets Smarter Over Time`

## 4. How It Works Section

### Step 1: Create Your Brand Profile
Answer 5 questions about your brand. AI will read your website and create your Brand DNA in 30 seconds.

### Step 2: Plan Your Shoot
Book photography or video. Your photographer gets a one-page brief with all technical specs and brand guidelines.

### Step 3: AI Routes Everything
Upload assets. AI tags every product, generates descriptions, and routes assets to the right channels automatically.
